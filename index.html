About Us

We are a group of students brought together by the belief that small, intentional actions can create lasting warmth. This stall is not just about food or crafts, but about presence, care, and community.

Our Mission

No plastic. Minimal waste. Maximum warmth.

Every choice we make reflects our commitment to sustainability, simplicity, and respect for our surroundings. We replace excess with intention, packaging with responsibility, and transactions with human connection.
